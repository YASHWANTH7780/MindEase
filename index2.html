MindEase is an app that enables users to record their thoughts, feelings, and moods using a mood scale, and helps them gain a better understanding of their subconscious behavioral or non-behavioral patterns over time. By providing valuable possibilities to help make your day better, MindEase seeks to assist people in attaining a better day, week, month, or year.

  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Mindfulness meditation
  • Journaling
  • Aromatherapy
  • Yoga Nidra
  • Art therapy
  • Creative expression
  • Acts of kindness
  • Positive affirmations
  • Float therapy

We have researched deep into the arts of physiotherapy used in ancient times to treat mental discomfort, and will be using these to help others achieve a state of mental wellness
